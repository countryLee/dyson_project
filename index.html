Immersive listening
with detailed audio
Hear music as the artist intended
Two powerful 40mm Neodymium speaker drivers go beyond the conventional range to reveal deep sub-bass and high-end brilliance, so you hear every note with clarity and detail. 
Best-in-class noise cancellation
Our 8 Active Noise Cancelling microphones cancel up to 40dB of unwanted noise 384,000 times a second, for fully immersive listening. So you don’t miss a beat.
Enhanced sound range - 6Hz to 21kHz
Most headphones operate on a frequency range from 20 to 20,000 Hertz. Our technology expands that range, from 6 Hertz at the low end to 21,000 Hertz at the top.

So they achieve greater clarity across the audible portion of the spectrum.
Enhanced sound range
Many headphones operate from 20–20,000Hz. We expand that range to 6–21,000Hz, for greater clarity and detail across the audible spectrum, for a more sensory experience of bass you can feel.
Energy-dense Neodymium magnets
Their high magnetic flux density creates a strong and uniform magnetic field for lower distortion and greater accuracy, delivering crisp treble, clear midrange, and deep, well-defined bass.

Masterfully tuned and balanced
Our loudspeaker drivers are left-right matched to 0.5dB to accurately recreate the recorded soundstage and reduce spectral smearing, for a more natural, realistic, and precisely balanced listening experience.
Digital signal processor for optimized audio
Our DSP optimizes the audio signal and equalises the speaker driver. This amplifies and attenuates the spectrum, and neutralizes harmonic distortion. So you can hear music the way the artist intended.
